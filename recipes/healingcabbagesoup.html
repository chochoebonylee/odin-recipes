Healing Cabbage Soup

Description

Whether you're feeling under the weather or you're just in the mood for a bowlful of comfort, warm up with this cozy cabbage soup recipe.

It's easy, tasty, and incredibly customizable (so you can keep it simple or get as creative as you want).

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, optional
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 (14.5-ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cups chopped cabbage
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large stockpot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add ground beef, onion, carrots and celery. Cook until beef has browned, about 3-5 minutes, making sure to crumble the beef as it cooks; stir in garlic, oregano, basil and red pepper flakes.
  3. Stir in tomato paste until well combined, about 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes, chicken broth, cabbage and bay leaf; season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  5. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer until flavors blend, about 15 minutes.
  6. Stir in parsley.
  7. Serve immediately.
